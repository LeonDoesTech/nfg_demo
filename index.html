Our Heritage

Established in 2023 by Noel Tan, NFG was born from a desire to redefine the local gym experience. Noel envisioned a space where professional-grade training environments could coexist with a welcoming, communal spirit. This is a place built on the foundations of persistence and peer support.

Over the years, Noel's Fitness Gym has grown from a personal project into a vital community hub. Our platform was designed to bridge the gap between our physical gym space and our members' digital lives, ensuring you always have access to your membership data.
